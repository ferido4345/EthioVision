Life Story
Tilahun Gessesse, also known as "Ethiopia's Voice," was a legendary Ethiopian singer and songwriter. Born on September 29, 1940, in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, Tilahun Gessesse's musical journey began at a young age. He developed a deep passion for music and honed his skills as a vocalist,
